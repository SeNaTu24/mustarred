## âœï¸ Adding a New Blog Post

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Log in** to the studio
2. Click **"Blog Post"** in the left sidebar
3. Click **"+ Create"** button (top right)
4. Fill in the form:

#### Required Fields:
- **Title**: Your article headline
- **Slug**: Click "Generate" button (creates URL automatically)
- **Category**: Choose from dropdown
  - Mustarred Insights
  - Compliance
  - Banking & Finance
  - Corporate Governance
- **Excerpt**: Short summary (shows on blog list page)
- **Content**: Full article text
- **Featured Image**: Click to upload or drag & drop image

#### Optional Fields:
- **Author**: Defaults to "Admin Mustarred" (change if needed)
- **Published at**: Defaults to now (change to schedule for later)
- **SEO Title**: Custom title for Google search results
- **SEO Description**: Custom description for Google

5. Click **"Publish"** button (top right)

**Done!** Your post is live on the website immediately.

## ğŸ†˜ Common Issues

### "Can't see Publish button"
- Make sure all required fields are filled (marked with *)
- Check for error messages in red

### "Image won't upload"
- Check file size (must be under 200MB)
- Try JPG or PNG format
- Check your internet connection

### "Post not showing on website"
- Make sure you clicked "Publish" (not just "Save")
- Check "Published at" date isn't in the future
- Refresh the website page

### "Lost my changes"
- Sanity auto-saves every few seconds
- Look for "Saved" indicator at top
- If unsure, click "Publish" to be safe
